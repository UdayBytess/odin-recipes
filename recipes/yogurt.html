Yogurt

Description

Yogurt is a healthy dairy food made by fermenting milk. It is rich in protein, calcium, and probiotics, which are good for digestion.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Yogurt starter culture
  • Sugar or honey (optional)
  • Fruits (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil the milk and let it cool slightly
  2. Add the yogurt starter and mix well
