Salad

Description

Salad is a fresh and healthy dish made using raw or cooked vegetables. It is light, nutritious, and easy to prepare.

Ingredients

  • Cucumber
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Lettuce
  • Salt and lemon juice

Steps

  1. Wash all vegetables properly
  2. Chop the vegetables into small pieces
  3. Add salt and lemon juice
  4. Mix well and serve fresh
